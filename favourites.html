FAVOURITE DRAMA

Photo Source

The people who are close to me can definitely tell I'm a big fan of Korean Dramas! But not the romance genre. I much prefer the action genre because it gives me such a rush of excitement and an adrenaline boost when I'm watching. One of my current obsessions is "A Shop For Killers", starring the incredible Lee Dong Wook and Kim Hye Joon. It tells the story of Jeong Ji An (Kim Hye Joon), who is the niece of Jeong Jin Man (Lee Dong Wook). When Jeong Ji An returns to her uncle's house after news of his death, she starts to uncover dark secrets, one by one, about his true, hidden identity. I seriously love this drama because it's so fast-paced, and every single episode is packed with amazing, interesting action scenes. Plus, it's only got 8 episodes, which is perfect for a quick binge!
