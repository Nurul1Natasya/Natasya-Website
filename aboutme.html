ORIGIN

My name is Nurul Natasya Binti Wahab but most people call me Natasya. I will officially turn 20 on August 8. I was born and raised in a village located in Bukit Payong, Kuala Terengganu. Terengganu is a state located on the east coast of Malaysia. This state is famous for its beautiful beaches and islands found here. Apart from the beaches, there are several other things that are also tourist attractions such as the Terengganu Drawbridge, various types of crackers such as 'keropok lekor', and historical places such as Batu Bersurat. I am the second child of three siblings. As most people say, the second child has a stubborn nature, and I do not deny it. When I was little I was a stubborn child and did not listen to my mother. Well, I was just a child then. Now I am stubborn too, but only when joking with my friends.
